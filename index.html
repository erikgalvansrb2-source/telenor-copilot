How it works

The map can show a 12 km coastal exclusion zone either from a land dataset (optional data/ne_110m_land.geojson) or from a polyline you draw along the coastline. Your position is checked against that zone to indicate if LTE activation is allowed.

For planning/visualization only. Geospatial buffers and datasets may be approximate.
